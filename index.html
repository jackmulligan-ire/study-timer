About Simply Study

A study timer that gives you only the minimal elements needed to get stuff done. Based on the Pomodoro TechniqueÂ®.

How does it work?

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute pause. Repeat the cycle 4 times. After the fourth study session, take a longer 15-minute break.

What makes this different from other study timers?

Simply Study is designed to not distract you from whatever you're doing. There's no to-do list and no reports.
